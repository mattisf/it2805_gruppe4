3. Purpose and Goals

The website's purpose is to expand the clients audience. The longer-term objective will be easier to achieve where the website will be strengthening the brand name, reaching out to and inform a larger portion of the target audience, and perhaps increasing sales.

The target group is both girls and boys, of all ages, that use jewelries. The brand currently only has foothold in Oslo, but by creating the website, the company firmly thinks that its audience will grow and spread to other regions of Norway.

4. Navigation Structure

The navigation structure of Frøken Oslo´s website can be described as clique, illustrated in site map in figure X.

The website has a hierarchy structure, as illustrated in the sitemap, with a top-down approach. The landing page for the website is the home page. Each top level page is referred too in the navigation bar / menu on the top of the layout on every page. Here you can access the sites home, shop and about, in addition to search and continue to your personal shopping cart. These pages represents each one category. To access the subcategory pages, a menu on the left site of the page will pop up for all belonging to the same category.

The hierarchical structure is established because it is the easiest to understand. That will improve the usability of the website. It is also scalable as it is easy to further develop and scale up the content of the website. The navigation bar on the top will ease the navigation throughout the use of the website. This structure is also a familiar structure on online shopping sites, which increases the reasons for choosing it.

5. Page Layout and Appearance

Our main focus, when coming up with the design, was to make the website appealing for the target audience. We wanted a clean design, where it was easy for the users to navigate through the website, as well as getting all necessary information. The navigation bar and their informative tabs makes that possible. The producer, Sigrid A. Hole, wanted a design that complimented the logo, and the type of designs off the products, that is why we chose the colors and layout as we did.

  • Font: The font we use at the webpage is “Darker Grotesque”.
  • Color Scheme:
  • Text: Black
  • Background: light pink (FCF6FC) on every page.
  • Images: The product images on the homepage will have a tiny border with color B49D9D. The product images on the shopping page will not have any border, but they will become bigger while hovering over them.
  • Body Width: 960px
  • Links/buttons: The logo in det navbar will be a clickable link, which will bring the user back to the homepage. The buttons that refers to shopping will have the color B49D9D. On every link or button, the mouse will change when the user hovers over it. The buttons will also get a shadow underneath, a darker shade of the applicable button, when hovering over.
  • Navbar position and its appearance: The navbar will be included at the top of every page. When the mouse hovers over the tabs, the mouse will change just like the mouse will with links and buttons. They will also turn a bit bigger when hovering over them.
  • Footer: Created by: Sigrid A. Hole. Contact: s.a.hole@frokenoslo.com
  • Background images: On the homepage we will use a bigger version of the logo with another lay out. On the other pages we will not use background images, because there will be product images and so on that are supposed to get all the attention.
  • Logo: Our logo will be placed at the top of every page, in the middle, with menu tabs on each side of the logo. The logo will as mentioned be clickable.

6. Content

There will be ten separate pages as follows:
  • Homepage – homePage.html
    • This will be the page that appears first when the users visit the website. This page will give users a preview of some of the products available on the site, as well as a short introduction to the brand and how it all started.
    • The page has a clean and appealing design. Although it includes some pictures and text, it will not get too overwhelming for the users. The users will get a taste of what the website is about, and because of the little amount of information on the homepage, the users will easily manage to navigate to the different pages in the navbar to see more of the products.
    • The three pictures on the homepage represents the three different types of jewelry that can be found on the website. They are placed in the centered in the body, and will have a width from 388-400px and a height of 480.77px.
    • At the bottom of the page there will be a box containing some information about the brand, with a short text and an artsy image representing the brand.
  • Shop – shop.html
    • This page includes every product that the brand has to offer. The users can scroll through all the products and see information about the price and the name of the product. They also get the opportunity to read more about the product by clicking a button that takes them to the product information site of the specific product.
    • The users can also sort the products after “most popular” and prices. In addition, they can also turn to three different pages which only includes the types of products they are interested in.
  • Shop “Rings” - rings.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “shop”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
  • Shop “Charms” - charms.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “shop”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
  • Shop “Necklaces” - necklaces.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “shop”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
  • Product Information – productInformation.html
  • Shopping Cart – shoppingCart.html
    • The shopping cart will have a design that matches the theme of the brand as well as the whole website. The user will get an overview of what they are about to buy, with an image of the products they are buying as well as the most important information, including the price and quantity of the products.
    • The user has the opportunity to add a promo code, and they get information about the shipping methods. When they choose to go to checkout, by clicking the “checkout”-button, they are being transferred to an external site for paying for the products.
    • The image of the product(s) will have a height of 197px and a width of 228px.
  • About “Our history” – history.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “about”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
    • This page will provide the users with more information about the brand, and how and why Sigrid A. Hole invented it. It also contains information about the different sorts of products available.
    • The page will help the users understand the meaning of why the brand was invented.
    • The page has a clean and easy design, and the text is not too long so that the users actually read it.
  • About “The Products” – production.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “about”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
    • This page will have the same lay out as the history page. The page contains a short text about the jewelry and informs the users on the products intention.
    • As with the other about pages, the text is minimized so that the reader is not overwhelmed.
  • About “Contact Us” – contact.html
    • This is one of three pages under the “about”-tab in the navigation bar at the top of each page.
    • This page provides the users with links and information on how to contact the brand employees, as well as links to the different social medias where the user will get evenly updates on the products available. The user can both start a chat or send an email with questions that might arise.

7. Minimum Requirements

  • Add items to cart
    The user needs to be able to add and remove items from their shopping cart, which will be handled in JavaScript.
  • Roll-down shop menu
    Interactive roll-down menu that allows the user to easily navigate between the different shop subcategories.
  • Script for displaying products
    Display products and sort them based on price and popularity.
  • Animated image slide show
    Image slide show showcasing products in shop subcategories, images switch on their own and and can also be changed by user.
