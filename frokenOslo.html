Our Client

Client Name: Frøken Oslo
Contact Person: Sigrid Agnete Hole

“Frøken Oslo” is a Norwegian jewelry brand established by the producer itself, Sigrid A. Hole. The Oslo based designer started creating accessorizes to herself and her friends during Covid-19. It didn't take long for her desk to be covered in a scattering of vibrant stones and pearls. The response was enormous and increased immediately. That's when the notion of starting her own brand first emerged. The new brand was introduced in January 2022 after months of diligent design, prototyping, idea development, and other preparations. Today, the collection consists of chains and rings, all of which may be mixed and matched to create custom jewelry. All of them combine beautiful design with a reasonable price point to appeal to both younger and more senior generations. Frøken Oslo firmly believes that all of the things they design, create, and sell should combine beauty and excellence.

Purpose and Goals

The website's purpose is to expand its audience. The longer-term objective will be easier to achieve where the website will be strengthening the brand name, reaching out to and inform a larger portion of the target audience, and perhaps increasing sales.

Audience

The target group is both girls and boys, of all ages, that use jewelries. The brand currently only has foothold in Oslo, but by creating the website, the company firmly thinks that its audience will grow and spread to other regions of Norway.

Content of the Website and Organization

  1. Home
    • Landing page
  2. Shop
    • Chains
    • Rings
    • Shopping Cart
  3. About
    • Our history
    • Product information
    • Contact information

Requirements

Functional

  • The website shall accept customer orders
  • The website should be adopted to the screen of the iPhone 6s and above
  • The product photos should have the option to zoom in
  • Product information shall be shared on social media like Linkedin, Facebook and Instagram

Non-functional

  • The products are in focus
  • The website is user-friendly and easy to navigate
  • A customer should easily find the right product
  • Every page of the website must load in less than 3 seconds

Final Location of the Website

The domain name will eventually be www.frokenoslo.com.

Technical Difficulties

We plan to post many pictures in the gallery sections of our website, so we may run into the technical problem regarding slowly-loading webpages. Such a website will be frustrating for users, so we will need to make sure to avoid such a problem by being conscious about the size and number of the photographs we use.
